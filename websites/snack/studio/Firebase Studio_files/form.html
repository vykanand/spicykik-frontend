Android Studio Cloud, accessed through Firebase Studio - Join the Waitlist

Android Studio Cloud, accessed through Firebase Studio enables you to conveniently open Android Studio projects directly in your web browser, hosted on Firebase Studio. Whether it's exploring sample projects or accessing existing Android app projects on GitHub, this feature streamlines your workflow by eliminating the need for local installations.

We are launching the Trusted Tester Program to provide a small number of developer teams early access to the service to gather feedback and improve functionality. If you and your team are interested in registering for the program, please fill out and submit the form below.

Information you submit in this form will not be shared outside of Google and will be deleted when the Trusted Tester Program concludes.
