Quick answers to common questions — same info as our chat assistant.
FAQ

How can I track my orders?

Track recent orders and their statuses on the My Orders page. For live support, use the chat widget or message us on WhatsApp.

Is Cash on Delivery (COD) available?

COD is available in selected pin codes across India. Enter your pincode at checkout to confirm payment options for your address. If unsure, ask via the chat widget.

What are the delivery timelines?

Orders typically ship within 1–3 business days. Delivery usually takes 3–7 business days depending on location and courier availability. Estimated delivery is shown at checkout.

How do I change an order?

If you need to change your order (items, address or quantity), contact us immediately via WhatsApp with your order number and requested changes. We'll try to update the order before it ships.

How do I cancel an order?

To cancel an order, contact us as soon as possible via WhatsApp with your order number. If the order has not yet shipped we can cancel and refund. If it's already shipped, follow the returns instructions.

Where do I apply discount codes?

Apply discount codes in the Discount code field at checkout and click Apply. Only one discount code is allowed per order and some promotions may have conditions.

Need more help?

Use the chat widget at the bottom-right or message us on WhatsApp for immediate assistance.
